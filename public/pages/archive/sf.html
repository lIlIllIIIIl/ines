NOSTALGIA ILLUSTRATIONS

SAN FRANCISCO NOSTALGIA ILLUSTRATIONS

As I went to United-States (more precisely in San Francisco) when I was younger, and I was nostalgic about it, I wanted to make something that would remind me of it. After seeing Louis Vigreux’s Sydney illustration , I wanted to do something similar, but of course with San Francisco. It was also the occasion to try something new and absolutely unknown: illustration.
